Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026

AudioMicSync ("the App") is developed and maintained by Kriaa. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. No Data Collection

AudioMicSync does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with Kriaa or any third party. The App does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any other network calls outside of the local network transfer described below.

2. Audio Recording

The App records audio from the device's microphone (including connected USB microphones) when triggered by a UDP command from software running on your local network. Recorded audio is stored as a WAV file in the App's private storage directory on the device.

Audio data is transferred only to the IP address and port that you configure in the App's settings. This is typically a receiver application running on a PC on the same local network. No audio data is ever sent to Kriaa or any external server.

3. Network Access

The App uses your device's Wi-Fi or Ethernet connection solely to:

  • Listen for UDP trigger commands on a port you configure (default: 9877)
  • Upload recorded WAV files to the PC IP address you configure (default port: 9878)

All network communication happens on your local network between your Android device and a PC you control. No data leaves your local network.

4. Permissions Used

  • RECORD_AUDIO — required to capture audio from the microphone.
  • INTERNET — required to transfer recorded files to your PC over the local network.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE — required to keep the recorder running reliably in the background.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — used to show a persistent status notification while the service is active.
  • WAKE_LOCK — prevents the device from sleeping while listening for triggers.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE / ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — used to display your device's local IP address in the UI.

5. Local Storage

Recorded WAV files are saved to the App's external files directory (Android/data/in.kriaa.audiomicsync/files/takes/). These files remain on your device until you delete them. The App does not back up these files to any cloud service.

6. Children's Privacy

AudioMicSync is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
