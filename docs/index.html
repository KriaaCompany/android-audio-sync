AudioMicSync

Android app that records audio from a USB microphone and syncs takes to your PC — triggered over Wi-Fi via Rokoko or any UDP source.

UDP Trigger

Listens for record_start / record_stop commands on a configurable UDP port.

USB Mic Support

Selects from available input devices including USB microphones.

Auto Transfer

Converts PCM to WAV and uploads to your PC receiver over HTTP on the local network.
